# Vendly: AI-Powered Social Commerce Ecosystem

Vendly is a comprehensive commerce solution designed to turn social media conversations into successful transactions. It combines conversational AI with professional business management tools to automate the consultative sales process for modern vendors.

## 🚀 The Core Vision
Vendly simplifies selling on chat platforms (Telegram, WhatsApp) by providing an "AI Employee" that handles customer interactions 24/7, while giving vendors a high-end dashboard to manage their entire operation.

Vendly is an AI-powered social commerce ecosystem that automates the transition from chat-based inquiries to confirmed sales for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). We offer an integrated suite of services including a consultative AI sales assistant that handles real-time negotiations and automated order capture on platforms like Telegram and WhatsApp, alongside a professional vendor dashboard for unified inventory management, financial tracking through a digital wallet, and deep business analytics. Our platform is specifically targeted at social media vendors and modern retailers who rely on consultative, chat-based selling and require a scalable infrastructure to manage high-volume customer interactions and secure payment processing without increasing operational overhead.
---

## 🏗️ Project Components

### 1. The Conversational AI (Sales Agent)
*   **Omnichannel Support**: Operates directly within Telegram and WhatsApp via intelligent webhooks.
*   **Consultative Selling**: Real-time product lookup, technical spec explanation, and FAQ handling.
*   **Negotiation Engine**: Smart "haggling" logic that allows the bot to offer discounts within vendor-defined limits to close sales faster.
*   **Order Generation**: Automatically captures customer details and creates orders within the system.

### 2. The Vendor Dashboard (Web Application)
*   **Analytics Engine**: Real-time visualization of sales trends, revenue, and customer engagement metrics.
*   **Product Studio**: Full-featured inventory management supporting variants (size, color), high-res images, and technical specifications.
*   **Customer Profiles**: Consolidated views of unique shoppers, their purchase history, and lifetime value across all channels.
*   **Financial Suite**: A digital wallet for managing earnings, tracking individual transaction ledger entries, and initiating bank payouts.
*   **AI Control Center**: Interface to toggle the AI agent, customize its personality, and set strict haggling rules.

### 3. The Core Engine (Backend Infrastructure)
*   **High-Concurrency API**: Powered by FastAPI for rapid response times and multi-tenant isolation.
*   **Payment Gateway**: Seamless integration with Interswitch for secure, localized payment processing.
*   **Data Layer**: Robust PostgreSQL database managed via Prisma ORM for reliable transaction handling.
*   **Logistics Ready**: Built-in support for integrating third-party delivery services (GIGL, Gokada).
