## Design Context

### Users
Vendors and small business owners in emerging markets (starting with Nigeria) who use WhatsApp and Telegram for sales. They need a reliable, "set and forget" digital employee that increases their revenue without being technically overwhelming.

### Brand Personality
**Trustworthy, Accessible, Intelligent.**
Vendly should feel like a high-end financial tool (Stripe) rather than a experimental AI toy. It should exude confidence and reliability.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Stripe-Inspired Polish.**
- **Consistency**: High-precision spacing and grid alignment.
- **Typography**: Clean, varied weights of Nunito (or similar) to differentiate hierarchy.
- **Color**: Purposeful use of primary green as an accent, not a dominant wash. Clean neutrals.
- **Themes**: Expertly balanced light and dark modes.
- **Anti-Reference**: No "vibecoded" AI glows, purple gradients, or unnecessary animations.

### Design Principles
1. **Utility First**: Every UI element must serve the goal of helping the vendor sell more.
2. **Invisible Intelligence**: AI features should be integrated as functional tools, not decorative elements.
3. **Precision Rhythm**: Use generous but structured white space to create a "premium" feel.
4. **Immediate Trust**: Use refined borders, subtle shadows, and crisp icons to signal production-grade quality.
